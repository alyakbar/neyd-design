Overview

A movement rooted in
North Eastern Kenya

NEYD Kenya was established to address the development gap facing youth in the North Eastern counties. We believe that young people are not just beneficiaries of development — they are its most powerful agents.

Through localized programs in education, tech, and civic participation, we are building a robust ecosystem of youth leadership that drives sustainable progress from Mandera to Lamu.
