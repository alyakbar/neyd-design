Through university chapters, volunteer networks, and strategic partnerships, we design and deliver programmes that are community-driven, evidence-based, and sustainable.

How We Work

Our Theory of Change

We believe that equipping young people with knowledge, skills, and opportunities creates a ripple effect of sustainable development across the region. Here's how our model works.

01

Access & Exposure

Providing foundational literacy, tech hubs, and safe learning spaces for marginalized youth.

02

Skill Building

Intensive training in digital skills, leadership, and civic participation via workshops and camps.

03

Action & Agency

Youth launch local initiatives, form university chapters, and engage with community leaders.

04

Systemic Change

A generation of empowered leaders driving economic growth and social cohesion in N.E. Kenya.
